## Warm-up

1. “Walk me through the last AI tool your team paid for voluntarily.” _(open)_  
2. “Who clicks ‘Approve’ on that invoice?” _(process)_  

## Problem discovery

1. “Tell me about a surprise AI bill moment in the past 90 days.”  
2. “When that happened, whose job was it to investigate?” _(RACI)_  
3. “What tools/spreadsheets did they use?”  

## Concept test (neutral framing)

_Show live app or clickable prototype_

1. “If pricing were $__/mo seat, pause—would you allocate budget Y/N?”  
2. “Who else should talk to me?” _(referrals)_  

## Interviewer reminders

- Prefer **recent concrete stories** (“last time…”) vs hypotheticals.  
- Silence is allowed—give space after spend questions (people often undervoice shame).  
- Never promise savings numbers verbally without audit JSON reference.
